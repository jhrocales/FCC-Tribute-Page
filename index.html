Hero Himmel

The fictional hero from Frieren: Beyond Journey's End
who inspired lives in the real world.

Himmel, leader of the hero's party wearing a cape
with his party members.

Some of Himmel's real world and
personal life impact:

  • Heroic characteristics of Himmel influences the real world as related below Mr Hsu Rui-hsian, dubbed Long-Haired Bro by local media, was one of 17 civilians who stopped a knife-wielding man on a train near Taichung City Hall station in May. “Even if I had to do it again, I'd make the same choice,” said Mr Hsu, a fitness coach and powerlifter. “It’s what the hero Himmel would have done,” he added, quoting a line from popular anime Frieren: Beyond Journey’s End.

  • Himmel taught me in my personal life to be and do kindness no matter how small and trivial it is. These small acts of kindness may not change the whole world, but it can change the world of the one receiving the kind act.

  • Himmel is a character that enjoys set-backs and is not daunted by difficulties throughout their journey. He sees these hurdles as challenges and then rises to the challenge.

  • Himmel, even though he was not choosen by the "hero's sword" still choose to venture forward and defeat the demon king. He did so with a replica of the hero's sword, and now that he became the hero that saved the world, his fake sword became real due to his accomplishments.

"Change someone's life, even just in a small way."
-Himmel
